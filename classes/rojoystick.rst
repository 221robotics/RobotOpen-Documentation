ROJoystick
==================

ROJoystick provides access to any controller or joystick data transmitted by the driver station. ::



	ROJoystick usb1(1);


	usb1.leftX();
	usb1.leftY();
	usb1.rightX();
	usb1.rightY();
	usb1.btnA();
	usb1.btnB();
	usb1.btnX();
	usb1.btnY();
	btnLShoulder()
	btnRShoulder()
	lTrigger()
	rTrigger()
	btnSelect()
	btnStart()
	btnLStick()
	btnRStick()
	dPadUp()
	dPadDown()
	dPadLeft()
	dPadRight()
	auxOne()
	auxTwo()
	auxThree()
	auxFour()
